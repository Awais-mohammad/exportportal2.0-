About Our Business

Exportportal.pk is an online B2B platform aiming to be a comprehensive international marketplace specifically designed for Pakistani exporters , which may include Small and Medium-sized Enterprises (SMEs) and their counterparts covering all sectors and industries.

Export Portal

A country like Pakistan with such a large population, famous for its diverse industries with a quality products portfolio and continuously growing businesses, offers great opportunities for international traders. In contrast, Pakistani exporters still have very little penetration and presence in the e-commerce domain. Many companies do not even have an online marketplace or presence, which limits the opportunities and recognition for them. Industries that are located in remote areas have little to poor internet connectivity. Several SMEs and micro export organizations are doing business in decades old traditional ways such as contact based trading.
